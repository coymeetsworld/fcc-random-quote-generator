About Random Quote Generator

The Random Quote Generator was written by Coy Sanders to complete one of the four Intermediate Front-End Development Projects for FreeCodeCamp to earn the Front-End Development Certification.

This application generates a random quote when the quote section is clicked. It also creates a Tweet button that allows the user to tweet out the quote to their followers. The Twitter API is used to accomplish this and documentation can be found here.

Random Quote Generator

Click on the quote to generate a random quote from the memorable TV show The Office. The quote can also be tweeted out to your followers. Enjoy!
